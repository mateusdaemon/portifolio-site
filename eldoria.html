Gameplay

Eldoria

In a mystical 3D world with charming 2D sprites, you play as a farmer who inherits a rare, ancient magic. To uncover the truth behind your legacy, you must explore Eldoria, collect the Grimoires of Knowledge, and face the dangers that stand in your way.

Game Mechanics

In Eldoria, I had the opportunity to develop various gameplay mechanics that enriched my skills as a game developer. I implemented a quest system, allowing players to progress through the story by completing objectives. The real-time combat system includes spellcasting and potion usage, adding strategic depth to encounters. Additionally, I designed a resource collection mechanic tied to certain quests, encouraging exploration and interaction with the environment. To make the world feel more alive, I developed map interactions and programmed the behavior of animals, such as wolves, sheep, and boars, giving them unique movement patterns and responses to player actions.
