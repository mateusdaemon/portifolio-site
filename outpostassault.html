Gameplay

Outpost Assault

As the last astronaut standing, your mission is clear: defend your outpost from an unstoppable alien invasion! Armed with powerful laser weapons and explosive firepower, you must fight wave after wave of extraterrestrial threats.

Game Mechanics

In Outpost Assault, I was able to develop several key gameplay mechanics that enhanced my skills as a game developer. I implemented a complete animations Unity's Blend Tree, ensuring smooth and dynamic character animations. The game also features special effects and sound integration, enhancing the immersive experience throughout the gameplay. To provide a sense of progression, I developed a leveling system, where players can upgrade new abilities at each level, making them feel stronger to defeat enemies. Additionally, I had the opportunity to work with toon shaders to achieve a very cool visual style, and I applied post-processing effects to enhance the overall rendering quality of the game.
